## 1. My decision
The first thing that I want to self host is a method of photo storage as my family takes a lot of photos I want to be able to store them all and be able to easily access them and look through them. After looking at a few opting I settles with Immich. This is a piece of self hosted software that can be used to mange both photo and videos. I also used this one as it has a very good searching feature using AI to recognise people and objects making it easy to search through. It is also easy to share photos with other people.
